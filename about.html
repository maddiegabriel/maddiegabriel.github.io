About Maddie



I'm going into my third year of studies in the awesome Software Engineering Co-Op program at the University of Guelph. I love programming and tackling new projects! I also like to play (and coach) soccer, create (and teach) pottery, read good books, and hang out with my big family.

Currently I'm on my first co-op term at Sun Life Financial in downtown Toronto. As a Software Developer intern, I work on creating Sun Life apps for smart home devices like Amazon Alexa and Google Home! I also get the chance to prototype and demo my ideas to management. On the side, I am building some websites for small businesses in my community. At school, I'm the co-founder of Guelph Women in Computer Science (GWiCS) and am involved as a Peer Helper with Learning Services, a member of the Society of Computing and Information Sciences (SoCIS) and much more!
