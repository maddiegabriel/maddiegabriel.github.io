About Maddie



Hello! I'm a 3rd year Software Engineering Co-op student at the beautiful University of Guelph! I love programming and tackling new projects. I also like to play (and coach) soccer, create (and teach) pottery, read good books, draw comics and hang out with my siblings.

I just finished my first 8-month co-op term as a Voice Application Developer at Sun Life Financial in downtown Toronto. I worked on designing, developing and testing the Sun Life app on Amazon Alexa and Google Home. On the side, I attend Hackathons and build websites for small businesses in my community. At school, I'm the co-founder of Guelph Women in Computer Science (GWiCS) and am involved on campus as a Peer Helper with Learning Services, a member of the Society of Computing and Information Sciences (SoCIS) and much more!
