About Sun Life



Sun Life Financial is one of the largest life insurance companies in the world. They mainly offer financial advice as well as life/health insurance to their clients. This Canadian company was founded in Montreal in 1865, making it over 154 years old!

Sun Life has buildings in 12 countries all over the world, but for my co-op I worked from the global Sun Life HQ at 1 York Street in Toronto. My floor - known as Ignite Studio - was built just over a year ago. Ignite Studio is home to the teams working on Sun Life's biggest tech innovations - this includes the mySunLife mobile app and Sun Life's Ella for Google Home and Amazon Alexa (my team!).

I chose Sun Life because I was interested in learning to develop on this new voice platform and love the explorative approach the company takes towards technology. Plus check out the view!

My Role

Voice Application Developer


Overview
My main responsibility during my work term was to develop new features Sun Life's voice application, which is available for public use on both Google Home and Amazon Alexa. The app has 2 main functions: users can search for nearby healthcare providers covered by Sun Life, or log in securely to check their Sun Life account balances!

The work I completed this term falls under 3 main categories:

1) Software development for voice product delivery

  • Work on an agile team (the 'Wildcards') to develop the Google Home version of the Sun Life app
  • Write code for new features on the app + develop several new searching algorithms
  • Tech-debt tasks (code refactoring, scalability, etc.)
  • Help with testing as needed (writing unit tests, white-box testing, regression testing)
  • Assist VUI Developer in brainstorming, discussing and implementing design decisions
  • Participate in Wildcards scrum ceremonies (daily standup, sprint refinement/review/retrospective)
  • Run code walkthrough sessions to train new co-ops + full time employees
  • Quick turnaround - I contributed code for 4 major app releases during my term
2) Demo our product to management/clients
  • Present demos of our app on both Google Home and Alexa
  • Help with French demos since I'm the only French-speaking member of the voice teams!
  • Some of the demos I've completed:
    • Ontario Ministry of Finance
    • Jacques Goulet, President of Sun Life Canada
    • Kevin Dougherty, Executive Vice-President of Innovation
    • Alice Thomas, Chief Digital Technology Officer
3) Innovation Projects
  • Ontario Ministry of Finance
