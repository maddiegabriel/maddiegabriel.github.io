Hank's History


Growing up in Toronto with 7 siblings, hotdogs were a weekly staple in my mum's kitchen. Some of my fondest memories feature a plate full of freshly grilled, boiled or steamed dogs - even my own wedding!

After my mum passed away in 1972, I decided to quit my job in construction to pursue my true passion: hotdogs!

And thus began Hank's Heavenly Hotdogs. For 48 years, I've been proudly serving up delicious, creative hotdogs to my loyal Toronto community - and I'll continue to do so until my arms get too old to flip the weiners!

With love and gratitude,

Hank Kielbasa,
Founder & Owner of Hank's Heavenly Hotdogs.

About Hank's

Hank's Heavenly Hotdogs is Toronto's favourite gourmet hotdog shop! Since 1975, Hank Kielbasa has been serving up the hottest dogs in town. Featuring creatively flavoured dogs, local craft beer and indoor/outdoor seating, there's no better place to grab a bite! Our daily specials are a hit, keeping hotdogs affordable for everyone.
